📰 Application 6: Hindi News Classification

Learn how Natural Language Processing automatically classifies Hindi news articles into categories such as Sports, Politics, Business, Technology, and Entertainment.

📖 Overview

Hindi News Classification is an NLP application that automatically assigns Hindi news articles to predefined categories based on their content.

Common news categories include:

  • 🏏 Sports
  • 🏛 Politics
  • 💼 Business
  • 💻 Technology
  • 🎬 Entertainment

This application is highly relevant to the repository theme because it demonstrates the complete NLP pipeline for Hindi text classification.

🧠 Relevant NLP Concepts Used

🧹 Text Preprocessing

  • Tokenization
  • Stop-word Removal
  • Lemmatization

📊 Feature Engineering

  • Bag of Words (BoW)
  • TF-IDF

🤖 Language Representation

  • Word2Vec
  • FastText

❓ Why These Concepts Are Needed

A machine learning model cannot directly understand raw Hindi text. Before classification, the text must undergo several NLP processing steps.

  • 🧹 Clean the raw text.
  • 🔤 Convert sentences into tokens.
  • 📊 Transform words into numerical representations.
  • 🤖 Use a classification algorithm to predict the news category.

These preprocessing and feature engineering techniques enable the model to recognize meaningful patterns and accurately classify Hindi news articles.

🚀 Expected Benefits

📂 Automatic Content Organization

Automatically categorizes thousands of news articles without manual intervention.

⚡ Faster News Publishing

Reduces the time required to organize and publish news content.

🎯 Better Recommendation Systems

Improves personalized news recommendations by grouping similar articles together.

📈 Improved Search and Navigation

Makes it easier for users to discover news based on their interests.

💡 Example

Input

"भारतीय क्रिकेट टीम ने विश्व कप जीता"

Predicted Category:

✅ Sports

Input

"संसद में नया विधेयक पारित हुआ"

Predicted Category:

✅ Politics

The NLP system analyzes the content, extracts meaningful features, and predicts the appropriate news category using machine learning models.

📌 Conclusion

Hindi News Classification is one of the most practical applications of Natural Language Processing. By combining preprocessing techniques, feature engineering methods such as TF-IDF, and language representations like Word2Vec and FastText, NLP systems can accurately categorize Hindi news articles. This improves content organization, recommendation systems, and user experience across digital news platforms.
