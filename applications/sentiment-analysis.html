😊 Application 3: Sentiment Analysis

Learn how Natural Language Processing is used to determine whether a piece of text expresses a positive, negative, or neutral opinion.

📖 Overview

Sentiment Analysis is an NLP technique used to identify whether a piece of text expresses a positive, negative, or neutral opinion.

It is widely used to analyze customer reviews, social media posts, product feedback, surveys, and public opinions.

🧠 Relevant NLP Concepts Used

🧹 Text Preprocessing

  • Text Cleaning
  • Stop-word Removal
  • Tokenization

📊 Feature Engineering

  • Bag of Words (BoW)
  • TF-IDF

🤖 Language Representation

  • Word Embeddings
  • Contextual Embeddings

❓ Why These Concepts Are Needed

Raw customer reviews and feedback often contain noisy information, informal language, and unnecessary words.

Positive Review

"यह मोबाइल बहुत अच्छा है।"

The model should classify this review as:

✅ Positive Sentiment

Negative Review

"यह सेवा बहुत खराब है।"

The model should classify this review as:

❌ Negative Sentiment

Text preprocessing removes noise, feature engineering extracts important information, and language representation captures semantic meaning, enabling accurate sentiment prediction.

🚀 Expected Benefits

💬 Customer Opinion Analysis

Helps organizations understand how customers feel about products and services.

📦 Better Product Development

Customer feedback helps companies improve product quality and introduce new features.

📈 Improved Business Decisions

Provides valuable insights for marketing, sales, and customer support strategies.

⭐ Enhanced Customer Satisfaction

Allows businesses to identify and resolve customer issues quickly.

💡 Example

An e-commerce platform collects customer reviews written in Hindi.

Review 1

"यह मोबाइल बहुत अच्छा है।"

Predicted Sentiment:

✅ Positive

Review 2

"यह सेवा बहुत खराब है।"

Predicted Sentiment:

❌ Negative

The sentiment analysis system helps businesses understand customer opinions and improve their products and services.

📌 Conclusion

Sentiment Analysis is one of the most widely used applications of Natural Language Processing. By combining text preprocessing, feature engineering, and language representation techniques, NLP systems can accurately classify opinions expressed in Hindi text, helping organizations make better decisions and enhance customer experiences.
