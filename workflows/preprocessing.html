⚙️ Complete Text Preprocessing Pipeline

Understand the complete workflow of preprocessing Hindi text before performing feature engineering and text classification.

🖼 Workflow Diagram

Figure 1: Complete Text Preprocessing Pipeline for Hindi Text Classification

📖 Workflow Explanation

Step 1: Raw Hindi Text

This is the original text collected from various sources such as:

  • News Articles
  • Blogs
  • Social Media Posts
  • Government Documents

Example

"भारत ने विश्व कप में शानदार जीत हासिल की है!!! 😊"

Step 2: Sentence Segmentation

The document is divided into individual sentences so that each sentence can be processed independently.

Example

भारत ने विश्व कप में शानदार जीत हासिल की है।

पूरे देश में खुशी का माहौल है।

Step 3: Tokenization

Each sentence is divided into individual words called tokens.

Example

भारत | ने | विश्व | कप | में | शानदार | जीत | हासिल | की

Step 4: Case Normalization

English words appearing in Hindi text are converted into a consistent format.

Example

"NLP", "nlp", "Nlp"

⬇

"nlp"

This ensures consistency throughout the dataset.

Step 5: Noise Removal

Unwanted elements are removed, including:

  • Emojis
  • URLs
  • Hashtags
  • Special Characters
  • Extra Spaces

Example

भारत ने मैच जीता!!! 😊

⬇

भारत ने मैच जीता

Step 6: Text Cleaning

Additional cleaning and formatting operations are performed to produce standardized and machine-readable text.

Step 7: Stop-word Removal

Frequently occurring words that contribute little meaning are removed.

Example

Original:
भारत ने क्रिकेट मैच में शानदार जीत हासिल की है
After Stop-word Removal:
भारत क्रिकेट मैच शानदार जीत हासिल

Step 8: Stemming / Lemmatization

Words are converted into their root or dictionary forms to reduce vocabulary size.

Examples

खेलते → खेल

बच्चों → बच्चा

Step 9: Processed Text

The final cleaned text is now ready for feature engineering and machine learning algorithms.

Example

भारत क्रिकेट मैच शानदार जीत

⭐ Importance

The Complete Text Preprocessing Pipeline removes noise, standardizes textual data, reduces vocabulary size, and prepares high-quality input for feature engineering and classification models.

Effective preprocessing significantly improves the performance, accuracy, and efficiency of Hindi Text Classification systems.
